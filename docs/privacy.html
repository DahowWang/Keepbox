Keepbox 隱私權政策

最後更新：2026 年 6 月 23 日

Keepbox（以下稱「本 App」）非常重視您的隱私。本政策說明本 App 如何處理您的資訊。簡而言之：本 App 不蒐集、不上傳、不追蹤您的任何個人資料。

我們不蒐集資料

本 App 不會蒐集、儲存或傳輸任何可識別您身分的個人資料。我們沒有伺服器來接收您的資料，也不要求您註冊或登入帳號。

您的內容儲存在哪裡

  • 您匯入或儲存的 HTML 檔案、資料夾、標籤等所有內容，僅儲存在您的裝置本機（透過 Apple 的本機資料庫）。
  • 這些內容不會離開您的裝置，也不會傳送給我們或任何第三方。
  • 若您使用 iCloud 裝置備份，這些資料可能依您自己的 iCloud 設定被納入 Apple 的備份，此行為由 Apple 與您的系統設定控管，與本 App 無關。

分享功能（Share Extension）

當您從其他 App 透過「分享」將網頁存入 Keepbox 時，本 App 僅讀取您主動分享的該筆 HTML 內容，並儲存在您的裝置上。本 App 不會存取您分享內容以外的任何資料。

網頁內容的呈現

本 App 使用系統內建的網頁元件（WebView）來呈現您儲存的 HTML。若您儲存的某個網頁本身含有指向外部資源（例如圖片、字型）的連結，開啟該頁時可能會依該網頁的內容向外部伺服器請求這些資源。這是該網頁原始內容的行為，本 App 不會主動傳送您的個人資料。建議僅儲存您信任來源的網頁。

第三方

本 App 不含任何分析工具、廣告 SDK 或第三方追蹤元件。

兒童隱私

本 App 不會蒐集任何使用者資料，適合所有年齡層使用。

政策變更

若本政策有所更新，我們會修改本頁面並更新上方的「最後更新」日期。

Keepbox Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Keepbox ("the App") takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains how the App handles your information. In short: the App does not collect, upload, or track any of your personal data.

We Don't Collect Data

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. We have no servers to receive your data, and we do not require you to create an account or sign in.

Where Your Content Lives

  • All content you import or save — HTML files, folders, tags — is stored only locally on your device (via Apple's on-device database).
  • This content never leaves your device and is never sent to us or any third party.
  • If you use iCloud device backup, this data may be included in Apple's backup according to your own iCloud settings. That behavior is controlled by Apple and your system settings, not by the App.

Share Extension

When you save a webpage into Keepbox via "Share" from another app, the App only reads the HTML content you actively shared and stores it on your device. The App does not access anything beyond the content you share.

Rendering Web Content

The App uses the system's built-in web component (WebView) to render the HTML you've saved. If a saved page itself contains links to external resources (such as images or fonts), opening it may request those resources from external servers as defined by that page's own content. This is the behavior of the saved page's original content; the App does not actively transmit your personal data. We recommend saving pages only from sources you trust.

Third Parties

The App contains no analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking components.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any user data and is suitable for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, we will revise this page and update the "Last updated" date above.
